## Helpers Helpers are methods that either come with Middleman or that you write that assist you in producing the output you want.
## Common helpers * `link_to` * `tag` and `content_tag` * `stylesheet_link_tag` * `javascript_include_tag` * `truncate` and `truncate_words` * `content_for` and `yield_content`

Make your own helpers


# in config.rb
require 'nokogiri'

helpers do
  def summarize(article)
    document = Nokogiri::HTML.parse(article.body)
    document.css('p:first').text
  end
end

Middleman data

Middleman makes any data at the top of a rendered page available to it. The data is in [YAML](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YAML).

Frontmatter

        
---
template: post
title: Quines!
tags: quines, javascript, lua
image: stairs.jpg
---

Local data

Middleman also makes data files stored under the data directory available to you.

data/menu.yml


Syllabus: /syllabus.html
Prework: /prework.html
Setup:
  Overview: /setup/index.html
  Atom: /setup/atom.html
  XCode: /setup/xcode.html
  Homebrew: /setup/homebrew.html
Resources: /resources.html
Notes: /notes/

layouts/layout.erb


      <% data.menu.each do |title, link_or_dropdown| %>
          ...
      <% end %>
